About Us

JayTech International was established as a proprietary tiny unit in the year 2010 and started manufacturing & supplying Valve & Pump parts. Precision Engineering Components were also manufactured for General Machine Tools, Textile parts, Auto parts, Flanges, Investment Casting parts, Forge parts. In the span of 10 years, it has expanded the manufacturing base with additional and sophisticated CNC machine tools and the latest inspection instruments. From the beginning, the turnover growth rate of the unit is increasing every year, is the result of team work of highly motivated, dedicated and quality conscious personnel.

Our Vision

To be a leader in the precision machining and casting pattern industry through innovations, quality products and mutually beneficial alliances.

Our Mission

  • To support our customers by providing superior products and services of exceptional value which help them gain competitive advantage in their markets.
  • To sustain our vision by constantly seeking renewal via continual improvement in processes, and the application of new technologies and best business practices.
  • To provide a pleasant, nurturing and growth oriented environment which encourages our employees to be highly productive and to grow personally and professionally.
