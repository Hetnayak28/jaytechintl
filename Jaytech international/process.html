Process

Flow Process- Die & Mould

Sr. No Particular Sequence of Activity
1 Quote to customer of his Inquiry as per his requirement Quotation
2 Drawing Sample and order with Schedule are Received from Customer Order / Planning
3 Job card release/Whole Designing And detail designing and preparing parts drawing Design / Release
4 Order to vendor for raw material requirement or order to customer for arrange Raw Material Order
5 Received raw Material(casting or bar or block) from customer / outside vender source Raw Material Inward
6 Checked raw goods and if require prepare pre-machining drawing Inspection
7 Send it to vendor or in-house conventional shop for pre-machining/part making with drawing Pre-machining (Conventional Shop)
8 Received it to VMC/CNC shop and checked pre-machining dimension CNC Shop / Inspection
9 Prepare programme and inspection report with set up sheet and clamping Programming/Setting
10 Inspect during machining after each programme In process Inspection
11 After completed VMC/CNC process send it for final inspection Inspection
12 Send it to conventional shop for Assembly(all part from VMC and conventional and readymade) Assembly
13 After Assembly completed polished goods Polishing
14 After polishing and Assembly inspect all thoroughly Inspection
15 If require, trial all Assembly Trial
16 Packing in boxes for Dispatch Packing
17 Dispatch with required Documents Dispatch

Flow Process-Machinery Components

Sr. No Particular Sequence of Activity
1 Quote to customer of his Inquiry as per his Requirement Quotation
2 Drawing Sample and order with Schedule are Received from Customer Order / Planning
3 Received raw Material(casting or bar or block) from customer / outside vender source Raw Material Inward
4 Prepare Job card & Checked raw goods and if require prepare pre-machining drawing Schedule / Designing
5 Send it to vendor or in-house conventional shop for pre-machining with drawing Pre-Machining (Conventional Shop)
6 Received it to CNC shop and checked pre-machining dimension Ready for CNC shop/Inspection
7 Prepare programme and inspection report for production as per job card Programming/Setting
8 First piece checked thoroughly after trial and before start production Trial/Inspection
9 After completed CNC process send it for final inspection Inspection
10 Send it to VMC shop(Vendor) if its require VMC/Milling process
11 After VMC completed deburred(polished) goods send it for hole and tapping Hole/Tapping
12 Hole And Tapping Done By Using Jigs And Fixture As Per Require Quality Hole/Tapping
13 After Finished All Operation, Polished And Cleaning And Deburred All Components Polishing/cleaning
14 Final Inspection Report Fill Up And Checked Final Inspection
15 Packing in Boxes for Dispatch Packing
16 Dispatch with required Documents Dispatch
