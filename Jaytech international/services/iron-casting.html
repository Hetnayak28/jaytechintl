Iron Casting

Durable and Versatile Iron Components Manufacturing

Overview

Iron casting is a specialized manufacturing process that produces components with superior strength, wear resistance, and durability. Our iron casting services include both gray iron and ductile iron casting, offering excellent thermal conductivity and vibration dampening properties.

Key Features

  • Excellent wear resistance
  • High strength-to-weight ratio
  • Good thermal conductivity
  • Superior vibration dampening
  • Cost-effective production
  • Wide range of sizes available
