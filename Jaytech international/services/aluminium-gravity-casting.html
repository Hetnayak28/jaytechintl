Aluminium Gravity Casting

Quality Aluminum Components Through Gravity Die Casting

Overview

Aluminium gravity casting is a process where molten aluminum flows naturally under gravity into a permanent mold. This method produces high-quality castings with excellent mechanical properties and is particularly suitable for medium to large production runs of aluminum components.

Key Features

  • Good mechanical properties
  • Excellent surface finish
  • Cost-effective for medium volumes
  • Consistent quality
  • Lower porosity than pressure die casting
  • Suitable for heat treatment

The Gravity Casting Process

1

Die Preparation

Cleaning and preheating the permanent mold

2

Coating Application

Applying release agent and thermal barrier coating

3

Metal Preparation

Melting and treating aluminum to required specifications

4

Pouring

Controlled pouring of molten aluminum into the mold

5

Solidification

Natural cooling and solidification of the casting

6

Extraction

Removing the solidified casting from the mold

7

Finishing

Trimming, heat treatment, and final inspection

Applications

Automotive

Cylinder heads, wheel hubs

Industrial

Pump housings, motor frames

Electrical

Motor housings, switchgear

Engineering

Machine components, structural parts
