Sand Casting

Traditional & Versatile Metal Casting Solutions

Overview

Sand casting is one of the most versatile and widely used metal casting processes. It involves creating molds from sand mixtures and pouring molten metal into them. This process is ideal for both small and large parts and can be used with nearly all metals and alloys.

Key Features

  • Cost-effective for large parts
  • Suitable for all metals
  • Low tooling costs
  • Flexible design options
  • Good for prototypes and production
  • Wide range of part sizes

The Sand Casting Process

1

Pattern Making

Creating a pattern in the shape of the final part

2

Mold Making

Packing sand around the pattern to create the mold

3

Core Setting

Placing cores to create internal cavities if needed

4

Pouring

Pouring molten metal into the sand mold

5

Cooling

Allowing the metal to solidify and cool

6

Shakeout

Removing the casting from the sand mold

7

Finishing

Cleaning and finishing the final casting

Applications

Heavy Industry

Machine bases, engine blocks

Automotive

Transmission cases, cylinder heads

Machinery

Gear housings, pump bodies

Marine

Propellers, engine components
