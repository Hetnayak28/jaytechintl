Powder Metal for Sintering Parts

Advanced Powder Metallurgy Solutions

Overview

Powder Metal Sintering is a sophisticated manufacturing process that creates high-performance metal components from metal powders through compaction and thermal processing. This technology enables the production of complex shapes with excellent mechanical properties and material efficiency.

Key Features

  • Near net shape capability
  • Minimal material waste
  • Excellent material properties
  • High dimensional accuracy
  • Cost-effective production
  • Wide material selection

The Powder Metal Sintering Process

1

Powder Selection

Choosing appropriate metal powders and additives

2

Mixing

Blending powders with lubricants and binders

3

Compaction

Pressing powder mixture in precision dies

4

Pre-Sintering

Initial heat treatment to remove binders

5

Sintering

High-temperature consolidation process

6

Secondary Operations

Optional sizing, machining, or heat treatment

7

Quality Inspection

Testing for properties and specifications

Applications

Automotive

Gears, bearings, bushings

Industrial

Filters, structural components

Tools

Cutting tools, wear parts
