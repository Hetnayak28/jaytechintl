Aluminium Pressure Die Casting

High-Precision Aluminum Components Manufacturing

Overview

Aluminium pressure die casting is a manufacturing process that forces molten aluminum into a mold cavity under high pressure. This process is ideal for producing high-quality, complex aluminum parts with excellent surface finish and dimensional accuracy at high production rates.

Key Features

  • High production rates
  • Excellent dimensional accuracy
  • Superior surface finish
  • Complex geometries possible
  • Thin wall capabilities
  • Cost-effective for high volumes

The Pressure Die Casting Process

1

Die Preparation

Cleaning and applying die lubricant

2

Metal Preparation

Melting and maintaining aluminum at optimal temperature

3

Injection

Forcing molten aluminum into the die cavity under high pressure

4

Solidification

Cooling and solidifying under pressure

5

Ejection

Opening the die and removing the casting

6

Trimming

Removing excess material and gates

7

Quality Check

Inspection and quality control

Applications

Automotive

Engine components, transmission parts

Electronics

Housings, heat sinks

Lighting

LED housings, fixtures

Industrial

Machine parts, equipment components
